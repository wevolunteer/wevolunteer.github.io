What is We Volunteer?

We Volunteer is an open-source platform developed by Metadonors and Produzioni dal Basso, designed to facilitate micro-volunteering. Originally created for the European Volunteering Capital 2024 in Trento, it's the ideal solution for organizations, universities, and institutions looking to promote volunteer activities within their network.

Our mobile app makes it easy to manage, search for, and participate in volunteer experiences. The platform integrates with the national CSV management systems to simplify administrative tasks, is federable across multiple installations, and ensures automatic insurance coverage for volunteers.

Platform Features

  • Built for Micro-Volunteering: Manage micro-volunteering via a mobile app for managing, searching, and participating in experiences.
  • Integrated with CSV Systems: Simplifies administrative tasks by integrating with national volunteer management systems.
  • Federable: Can be federated with other instances to align with specific brand strategies.
  • Insurance Integration: Automatically integrates with Generali systems to provide insurance coverage for registered volunteers.

Benefits for Volunteers

  • Easy participation in volunteer actions through a quick and simple process.
  • Assurance of comprehensive insurance coverage during volunteering.
  • Access to the Welion Card for free, and additional exclusive benefits offered by Generali to their volunteer clients (discounts, vouchers, etc.).
